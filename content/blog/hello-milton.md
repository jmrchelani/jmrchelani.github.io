# Hello, I am Milton

This is the first post in the new blog system.

The idea is simple: write posts as Markdown files, build the site, and publish real static pages that search engines can read without needing JavaScript.

## What I will write about

- Product ideas and build notes
- Software experiments
- Automation and developer tools
- Lessons from freelance and product work
- Small technical notes worth keeping public

The portfolio can stay playful and OS-inspired, while the blog can stay clean, readable, and indexable.
